AFP RWH coverage 2012

This is a summary of what parts of the Real World Haskell book students should know after the course (including some parts that students should know before the course).

Note that the AFP course also covers some aspects not covered by the RWH book: parts of lectures 2 (EDSL), 4 (program transformation), 7 (type based modelling), and the lectures on Feldspar, Agda and Lazy SmallCheck.

Summary:

  • Covered chapters: 1-7,8.6,9-11,13,14,15,18,19
  • Useful for the labs: 22,23
  • Cursory reading: 8,12,16,24,26,28
  • Not covered (but a good read!): 17,20,21,25,27
